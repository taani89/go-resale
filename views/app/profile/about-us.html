About Us

Purpose of GoReSale

GoReSale is a mobile application that was developed by Winterfall Development Team. GoReSale helps its user by providing the information about the awareness of green environment and using the green product to maintain the sustainability of our environment. Besides, users are allowed to buy and sell their green product through this mobile application.
